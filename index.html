About Me

Write something about me? Gosh, that sounds like you're asking me to write something for a dating site or something...

Hello! My name is Kieran Anthony, I tend to code under the moniker zekkxx, which has been my email since I was seven. I think I made it to play Neopets or something like that in elementary school. So I'm rather attached to it. I'm a new developer working on getting involved with the industry and currently am enrolled in the University of Minnesota Full Stack Developer Bootcamp. Just like the name begets, I'm interested in both front-end and back-end development. I have a bit more experience with backend applications simply because of my schooling, but I'm planning on making sure that I have the proper experience with front-end design too.

I'm a Minnesota native, and I currently live in Bloomington, not too far from Thomas Jefferson Senior High School. I'm looking for jobs in the Twin Cities, but I'm not against travelling outside of my hometown. I actually spent 4 years in Indiana attending Ball State University so I'm willing to go just about anywhere.

In my spare time, I work on independant projects like my homebrew version of Dungeons & Dragons that I call Saerie. I also like to play video games and am an amateur game developer as well. I am part of the Twin Cities IGDA in respect to that. I also like to swim, and I enjoy long walks in the park and on the beach.
